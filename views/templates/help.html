Controls

Mouse scroll and clicks in waveform area can be used to navigate the current track's progress. Mouse scroll handler is also registered on volume control.

You can start with adding a track to an empty playlist. Just drag a file from your hard drive and drop it in the playlist area.

In addition to that you can also add various online links to play them. You can simply add a link to a video on YouTube or a track on Soundcloud by pasting the link directly into the player.
